Description

A relaxing ritual before bedtime.

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon chamomile
  • 1 teaspoon ginger
  • 1 teaspoon mint

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil.
  2. Place plant material in a tea strainer basket.
  3. Place the strainer basket in an 8 to 12 ounce drinking mug.
  4. Pour the hot water into the mug.
  5. Cover mug and infuse for 15 minutes before drinking.
