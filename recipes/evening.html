Description

A relaxing ritual before bedtime.

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon chamomile
  • 1 teaspoon ginger
  • 1 teaspoon mint

Steps

  1. Boil enough water to fill an 8 to 12 ounce drinking mug.
  2. Put ingredients in a tea strainer basket.
  3. Place the strainer basket in the mug and fill with boiled water.
  4. Cover mug and infuse for at least 15 minutes before drinking.
