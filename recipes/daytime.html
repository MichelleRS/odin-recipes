Description

A moisturizing and nutritious blend.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon calendula flowers
  • 1 tablespoon marshmallow root
  • 1 tablespoon nettle leaves
  • 1 tablespoon plantain leaves

Steps

  1. Add plant material to a 32 ounce glass jar.
  2. Fill jar with cold or room temperature water.
  3. Stir to combine.
  4. Cover and store in refrigerator overnight.
  5. Strain and sip throughout the day.
